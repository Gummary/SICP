~~~scheme
(try try)

(if (halts? try try)
  (run-forever)
  ('halted))
~~~

假设try对try“终止”，则(try try)应返回'halted，

即(halts? try try)不成立，此时与halts?的定义矛盾

若try对try终止，则(try try)应run-forever,即(halts? try try)成立,也与halts?的定义矛盾
